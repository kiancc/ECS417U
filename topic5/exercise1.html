My Top 5 Tourist Attractions

The Peak

The Peak is the highest mountain in Hong Kong, with an altitude of 552 meters.

Vasa

Vasa is a Swedish warship that was built from 1626 to 1628. The ship sank on its maiden voyage in 1628.

Forbidden City

The Forbidden City is a palace complex in Beijing, China. It was the former Chinese imperial palace and state residence of the Emperor of China.

Le Jules Verne

Le Jules Verne is a gourmet restaurant located on the second floor of the Eiffel Tower in Paris, France.

Niseko

Niseko is a popular ski resort in Hokkaido, Japan. It is known for its powder snow and world-class skiing and snowboarding.
